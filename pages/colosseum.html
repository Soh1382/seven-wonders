The Colosseum

  • The Colosseum was built between 72 A.D and 80 A.D in ancient Rome
  • The Colosseum is the largest amphitheater in the world
  • This brilliant building had 80 entrances and could seat approximately 50,000 spectators
  • The first games ever to be held were in 80 A.D, under Emperor Titus
  • Below the Colosseum were numerous rooms and underground passages
