Taj Mahal

  • Taj Mahal is located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India
  • It stands 73 meters tall
  • Taj Mahal was built 1632-53 A.D
  • It was build for Mumtaz Mahal
  • The architect of Taj Mahal was Ustad Ahmad Lahauri
