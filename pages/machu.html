Machu Picchu

  • In the Quechua Indian language, “Machu Picchu” means “Old Mountain”
  • Machu Picchu is a 15th-century Inca citadel
  • Machu Picchu is located in the Eastern Cordillera of southern Peru
  • Machu Picchu is on a 2,430-meter (7,970 ft) mountain ridge
  • Machu Picchu was abandoned after 122 years being found
