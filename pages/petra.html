Petra

  • Petra is believed to have been established in 312 BC
  • Approximately only 15% of Petra has been explored by archaeologists
  • The name Petra is derived from the Greek word ‘petros’, which means rocks
  • Petra is home to roughly 800 tombs, therefore known as the “Royal Tombs”
  • A Swiss explorer called Johann Ludwig Burckhardt discovered Petra in 1812
